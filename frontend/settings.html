Sahayak (सहायक — meaning "helper" in Hindi) is an AI-driven platform built to connect India's 400+ million blue-collar workers with employers across industries. It supports multilingual voice and text conversations, real-time job matching, salary prediction, document verification via vision AI, and dispute resolution — all on a 100% free-tier AI stack.
